About Us

We are a group of students from an international school in South Korea,
interested in environmental issues and programming. This website is
part of our CAS project, which is one of the core programs of the Diploma Programme.
Our purpose is to raise awareness about different environmental issues through
this website, and please keep in mind that we are not a group of professional
researchers. Feel free to explore our website to explore different environmental topics.
